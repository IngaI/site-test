🏛 Landmarks, History & Attractions
Kalaja e Artanës (Novo Brdo Castle)
Medieval Fortress · 13th–14th Century · ~35km from PRN
Free · Asphalted road to top
A medieval fortress perched dramatically on a hilltop about 35km from Pristina (40 min drive) — dating from the 13th–14th century with possible Illyrian origins. The site was once one of the most populated areas in the medieval Balkans due to rich gold and silver deposits. The entire road is asphalted to the top. Reviews call it a must-see, especially at sunset when the castle and surrounding nature are at their most beautiful. Under partial restoration.
🏰 4.8/5 from 51 reviews. Go at sunset. Combine with a loop drive south of Pristina.
Pandora Castle
Event Venue · Castle · ~25km from PRN
Event/booking based
A charming castle-style event venue outside Pristina — primarily used for weddings and private events, but occasionally open for visits. 4.8/5 from 8 reviews. Reviewers call it "very beautiful" and note it's not a restaurant — arrangements must be made in advance. Check their website (Instagram: @pandoracastle.ks) for upcoming events. Note: Google Maps directions are reportedly wrong — ask the venue for correct directions when booking.
📞 +383 46 106 444. Worth checking if an event is running during your visit.
